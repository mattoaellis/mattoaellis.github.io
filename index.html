Biography

Dr Matthew Ellis is a Lecturer in Machine Learning and member of the Machine Learning Group at the Department of Computer Science at the University of Sheffield. My research interests are in developing energy efficient devices based on spin-electronic devices and reservoir computing. I have previously worked with Prof. Stefano Sanvito at Trinity College Dublin and Prof. Roy Chantrell at the University of York.
